https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/blog/overlay-contact-information-on-wallpaper/workflow-overlay-contact-information-on-wallpaper.png "Overlay Contact Information On Wallpaper"){.image-right} A while ago Katie Floyd put out a call on Twitter looking for a replacement app which can overlay contact information onto your wallpaper for iOS. I thought "I bet you can do this with Workflow", and I was right! Here's how it works.

Steps:

  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/Text.png "Text") Text: This contains your name.
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/Text.png "Text") Text: This contains your phone number, advice: Don't use the number of your iPhone, use that of a trusted family member or friend, or your home phone if you have one.
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/Text.png "Text") Text: HTML formatting of the above two items, your name is set to be smaller than the phone number as it's likely to be longer and not quite as important.
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/RichText.png "Make Rich Text from HTML") Make Rich Text from HTML: Now we make that HTML into rich Text - applying the formatting.
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/PDF.png "Make PDF") Make PDF: Bear with me, PDFs are great!
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/Image.png "Convert Image") Convert Image: PDFs can be converted to images - voila! We now have a white image with black text.
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/Image.png "Crop Image") Crop Image: Let's make this approximately the same aspect ratio as an iPhone X screen.
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/Image.png "Resize Image") Resize Image: Shrink it down to be about the right size
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/Variable.png "Set Variable") Set Variable: Make it easy to access in a moment.
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/Photos.png "Select Photos") Select Photos: Choose your background.
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/Image.png "Mask Image") Mask Image: Here's the magic. The mask image action is like a very smart overlay, here it will make sure the text of your name and contact number shows up on the wallpaper, and nothing else.
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/QuickLook.png "Quick Look") Quick Look: Check it for prettiness.
  • https://resources.rosemaryorchard.com/images/workflow-icons/Sharing.png "Share") Share: Save it where you want!
