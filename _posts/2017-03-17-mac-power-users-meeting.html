This week the Mac Power Users podcast hosted a meet up in Chicago. The event was sponsored by MacPaw (delicious food, thanks guys!), and it was a lot of fun.

Along with meeting Katie and David, I also met Tom Siko, Joshua McNary, Tony Marengo, and many others. Dr Drang was apparently in the room too, however as he was anonymous I couldn't say how much I appreciate his script sharing on his blog.

I didn't go just for the appetisers of course, it was lots of fun to meet everyone, and I even got a t-shirt!
